  • Device Information: The type of device you use the application on, operating system, unique device identifiers (e.g., device ID), IP address, and mobile network information. We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Communication: To communicate with you regarding event updates, important announcements, or your support requests.
  • Application Development and Improvement: To analyze application performance, fix errors, develop new features, and optimize the overall user experience.
  • Security and Fraud Prevention: To ensure the security of the application, detect, and prevent unauthorized access or malicious activities.
  • Legal Obligations: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, or court orders.

Your personal data will not be used for purposes other than those explicitly stated.

We take necessary technical and administrative measures to securely store the data we collect. Your data is hosted on secure and leading cloud platforms like Firebase.

  • Encryption: Encryption methods may be used during the transmission and storage of sensitive data.
  • Access Restrictions: Access to your data is granted only to authorized personnel.
  • Data Retention Period: Your data is retained for as long as necessary to provide the service, fulfill legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

However, no method of transmission or storage over the internet is 100% secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your data, but we use the best possible practices.

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Right of Access: To request access to the data we hold about you.
  • Right to Rectification: To request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to Erasure: To request the deletion of your data under certain conditions.
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: To request restriction of the processing of your data.
  • Right to Data Portability: To receive your data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format or to request its transfer to another data controller.
  • Right to Object: To object to certain processing activities of your data.

If you wish to exercise these rights or have any questions regarding your data privacy, please do not hesitate to contact us using the contact information below.
